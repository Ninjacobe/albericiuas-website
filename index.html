About AlbericiUAS

"The most powerful images are the ones that show you a place you know, in a way you've never seen it."

AlbericiUAS is a professional UAS (unmanned aerial systems) operation focused on cinematic-quality aerial videography and photography. Every project begins with a conversation about what you're trying to communicate — and ends with footage that says it clearly.

We operate under FAA Part 107 certification and coordinate with ATC for controlled airspace access, night waivers, and beyond-visual-line-of-sight operations when the project requires it. Fully insured. Equipment maintained to manufacturer specification.

Certification FAA Part 107
Insurance Fully Insured
Format 4K / 6K Cinema
Operations Day · Night · LiDAR

Aerial cinematography requires equal parts technical discipline and artistic judgment. That's what we bring to every flight.
